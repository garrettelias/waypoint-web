Waypoint Coming to Android

For photographers who plan before they shoot

The plan that's still in your pocket when you get there.

Waypoint is a field planning tool for photographers. Each stop on your shoot is a waypoint — a pinned location, a light window, and your own notes, ready before you leave the house. Navigate stop to stop. Log what worked.

  • 01 Plan your shoot as a sequence of waypoints — location, timing, and notes locked in before you leave.
  • 02 Arrive at the right time — build shooting windows into each waypoint so the light you planned for is the light you get.
  • 03 Move fluidly between stops on a multi-location shoot without losing your place or your plan.
  • 04 Track outcomes at each waypoint and build a personal record of your most successful shooting spots.
